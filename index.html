About Me

Once i was satisfied with how it worked I would move onto the next. Thus, started my journey towards computer science. I wanted to know how it all worked and during my high school decided to pursue it as my future field of study. Well now here i am trying to learn as much as possible and trying to achieve my goal of becoming a data scientist and working for some of the biggest companies in the world such as IBM, apple, google and so on. Other than this i love swimming whenever i get a chance, cook or even try food from different parts of the world and travel so I can learn about cultures throughout the world.
